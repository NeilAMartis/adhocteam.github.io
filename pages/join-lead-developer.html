Lead Developer

Location: DC, Baltimore, or Anytown, USA

If you have a background in product development, user experience design, or software development, and also love to shape and guide a product from idea to implementation, the Lead Developer position is for you. As a Lead Developer, you will be responsible for directing and influencing product design, development, and architecture. You will work closely with a small team of developers and designers (usually 2-5 people) to plan and prioritize features, and work closely with our public sector clients to understand their needs and involve them in the product development process. If you're technical but also love to direct a product as it grows, this position is for you.

Basic qualifications:
  • 5+ years of software engineering experience, software architecture, coding, testing and support.
  • 5+ years of full-stack web application development experience in any major open source language or framework such as Ruby/Ruby on Rails, Java, Golang, or Python; JavaScript, CSS and HTML.
  • Experience leading a team of developers, and using agile project management tools such as Pivotal Tracker, and version control tools, preferably Git.
  • Experience building secure and scalable cloud environments with Amazon Web Services.
Desired skills:
  • Consumer-facing web application design and development experience.
  • Experience with web accessibility, Section 508 compliance.
