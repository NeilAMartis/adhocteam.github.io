User Experience Designer

Location: Anytown, USA

We are redefining how citizens interact with government online. We need people to help us address real-world problems that affect millions of Americans. You are comfortable tackling a wide range of high-level UX tasks and have the ability to see the essential information required to quickly build rigorous UX process to the product development cycle. You can dig into user research to gain valuable insights, synthesize them into low-fidelity prototypes, and have the humility to collaborate with a diverse team to improve your work.

You will work both independently, and with other members of our team of designers, developers, researchers and stakeholders.

Basic qualifications:

  • Plan and facilitate user research sessions.
  • Create new online user experiences from existing offline and online experiences.
  • Prototype user flows using mockup tools such as moqups or Balsamic.
  • Perform task analysis and develop resulting task grids.
  • Define mental maps that help identify design opportunities and pain points.
  • Identify and create empathy maps from developed personas and design consistent user journeys and experience maps.
  • Define content models for form design.
  • Create process diagrams and basic wireframes.

Desired skills:

  • Familiarity with common project management tools such as Pivotal Tracker, Git, and GitHub.
  • Designing consumer-facing products and services.
