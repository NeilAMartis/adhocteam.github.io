Software Engineer

Location: Anytown, USA

We are looking for software engineers who love solving challenging technical problems and want their work to make a difference.

You will work with a small team to design, develop, and deliver web-based applications and services. You will work with our government partners to better understand and translate policy and business requirements into features, user stories, automated tests, and working code. Your work will impact the lives of millions of Americans.

Basic qualifications:

  • Developing and operating consumer-facing web applications and services.
  • Programming in a major open source language, such as Ruby, Python, Go, or Java.
  • Deep understanding of the fundamentals technologies of the web: HTTP, TLS/SSL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON.
  • Experience with common collaborative development tools, such as Pivotal Tracker, Git, and Jenkins.
  • Experience with MySQL or PostgreSQL.

Desired skills:

  • Experience with client-side JavaScript framework such as Angular, Ember, or React.
  • Experience with Amazon Web Services.
