Web Designer

Location: Anytown, USA

We want to hire designers with superlative experience creating simple and elegant online experiences. You will work with our teams of researchers, product developers, and software engineers to create mockups and prototypes with an emphasis on usability and accessibility.

You will work with a small team of engineers to design, develop, and deliver web-based applications and services. You will work with our government partners to better understand and translate policy and business requirements into features, user stories, automated tests, and working code. Your work will impact the lives of millions of Americans.

Basic qualifications:

  • Designing consumer-facing applications and services by creating mockups and comps.

Desired skills:

  • Implementing a design in HTML and CSS/SASS.
  • Experience with responsive front-end frameworks such as Foundation or Bootstrap.
  • Knowledge of accessibility and 508 compliance standards and tools.
  • Experience designing consumer-facing applications and services.
